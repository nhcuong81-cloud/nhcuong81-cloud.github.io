Privacy Policy - PMC220 Energy Monitor

Last updated: 2025

1. Introduction

PMC220 Energy Monitor (“we”, “our”, “the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

We do not collect, store, process or share any personal data from users.

2. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information such as name, email, phone number, location, device identifiers, or any personal data.

3. Bluetooth Permissions

The App uses Bluetooth only to connect to the PMC220 energy meter. The permission is used solely for:

  • Discovering Bluetooth devices
  • Connecting to the PMC220 device
  • Reading measurement data (voltage, current, power, etc.)

The App does not track your location or collect any personal information.

4. Data Usage

All measurement data is processed locally on your device. No data is uploaded or sent to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, tracking frameworks, or cloud services.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages and complies with COPPA requirements. No personal information is collected from children.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Since no data is collected or stored, there is nothing to delete. All information is temporary.

8. Security

All communication is done locally via Bluetooth. No data is transmitted over the internet.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary. New versions will be posted here.
